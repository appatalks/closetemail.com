How It Works

Getting your custom handle is simple and straightforward. Just follow these easy steps:

1. Submit Your Request

Fill out the request form below with your desired username and contact information.

Request Your Custom Handle Now

2. We Set It Up

Once we receive your request:

  • Verification: We'll verify the availability of your desired username.
  • DNS Configuration: We'll set up the necessary DNS records to associate your custom subdomain with BlueSky.
  • Confirmation: We'll send you an email with confirmation and further instructions.

3. Update Your BlueSky Handle

After receiving our confirmation email:

  • Log in to BlueSky: Access your BlueSky account settings.
  • Change Handle: Navigate to the handle settings and enter your new custom handle (e.g., @yourname.closetemail.com).
  • Verification: BlueSky will verify the DNS records, and your new handle will be active!
